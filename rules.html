Rules : How to play Simon Color Game

  1. Press the "Any Key" to begin the game.
  2. Simon will give the first signal.
  3. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color button.
  4. Simon will repeat the first signal and add a new signal.
  5. Repeat the sequence by pressing the buttons in the same order.
  6. Continue repeating the sequence as long as you can without making a mistake.
  7. Your score increases by one for each correct repetition.
  8. If you make a mistake, the game ends, and your score is displayed.
  9. The game starts with a sequence of one signal and gets longer with each correct repetition.
  10. Use the colored buttons to repeat the sequence.
